Why Qlocker

Security that doesn't slow you down

Every feature built around one principle — your data stays yours, always.

AES-256 Encryption

End-to-end encryption with military-grade AES-256 standard. Data is encrypted before it ever leaves your device.

Zero-Trust Access

Every request is verified. No implicit trust, no exceptions. Granular permission controls at the file, folder, and API level.

Real-Time Audit Logs

Every access event, timestamp and user action is logged immutably. Compliance made effortless with one-click exports.

Multi-Device Sync

Seamlessly access your secured data across all devices. Native apps for iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Team Collaboration

Secure sharing with fine-grained role control. Onboard or offboard team members instantly without compromising data.

Threat Detection

AI-powered anomaly detection monitors for unusual access patterns and automatically locks accounts under threat.
